# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Internal RGMC Group staff: IT developers, system admins, department heads, and general employees. Used daily in an office environment on desktop browsers. Developers use the kanban board and activity logs heavily; admins manage users, issues, and systems; regular staff file helpdesk tickets and check their workspace.

## Product Purpose

RGMC Gateway is the internal IT and operations portal for RGMC Group. It centralises issue tracking, developer task management, department administration, and helpdesk intake. Success means staff can file, track, and resolve issues without friction, and developers can manage their work in one place.

## Brand Personality

Capable, sharp, direct. The portal is a professional instrument — it should feel authoritative and controlled, not decorative. Gold on near-black gives weight without flashiness.

## Anti-references

- Flashy SaaS aesthetic (Notion, Linear) — overdesigned, too much whitespace theatre
- Generic admin templates (Bootstrap/Material panels) — bland, interchangeable
- Overly corporate enterprise grey (Jira, ServiceNow) — lifeless and demotivating
- Neon/gaming dark themes — RGB glow, too much visual noise

## Design Principles

1. **Instrument, not decoration.** Every visual element serves a function. Ornamentation that doesn't carry meaning gets cut.
2. **Gold earns attention.** The gold accent is reserved for active state, primary actions, and key data — never used as wallpaper.
3. **Dark serves focus.** The near-black surface reduces ambient distraction for people working in it for hours.
4. **State is never ambiguous.** Loading, empty, error, and success states are distinct and clear at a glance.
5. **Speed is respect.** Animations communicate state; they never delay or decorate.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Desktop-primary; responsive down to tablet.
